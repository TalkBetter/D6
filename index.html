📖 Part 3

Part 3: Reading Time

Reading Passage + Questions and Answers 閱讀與問答

Plants ~ Natures Helpers
植物～大自然的小幫手

Plants are wonderful living things!

植物是很棒的生物！

They come in many shapes and sizes, from tiny flowers to tall trees.

它們有很多不同的形狀和大小，從小小的花到高高的樹都有。

Plants grow in soil and need sunlight, water, and air to be healthy.

植物生長在土裡，並且需要陽光、水和空氣才能健康成長。

Plants make their food using sunlight.

植物利用陽光製造自己的食物。

This helps them grow strong and healthy.

這幫助它們長得強壯又健康。

Some plants make fruits and seeds.

有些植物會長出水果和種子。

When animals eat the fruit, they help spread the seeds so new plants can grow.

當動物吃水果時，牠們也會幫忙散播種子，讓新的植物長出來。

Some, like flowers, attract bees and butterflies, which help them grow.

有些植物，像花朵，會吸引蜜蜂和蝴蝶，而牠們也會幫助植物生長。

Plants are important for our world.

植物對我們的世界很重要。

They provide homes for animals, help clean the air, and make Earth more beautiful.

它們為動物提供家園、幫助清淨空氣，並讓地球更美麗。

❓ Q&A

Questions and Answers

讀完文章後，試著回答問題。

Question 1

What do plants need to be healthy?

植物需要什麼才能健康成長？

Answer

Plants need sunlight, water, and air to be healthy.

植物需要陽光、水和空氣才能健康成長。

Question 2

What do plants use to make food?

植物用什麼來製造食物？

Answer

Plants use sunlight to make food.

植物利用陽光來製造食物。

Question 3

Why is it important for animals to eat fruit from plants?

為什麼動物吃植物的果實很重要？

Answer

It is important because animals help spread the seeds so new plants can grow.

這很重要，因為動物會幫忙散播種子，讓新的植物長出來。

Question 4

How do plants help the world?

植物如何幫助這個世界？

Answer

Plants help the world by providing homes for animals and helping clean the air.

植物藉由提供動物棲息的家園以及幫助淨化空氣來幫助這個世界。

Part 4: Video Learning

Adjectives 形容詞影片教學

Adjectives describe a noun.

形容詞用來描述名詞。

They tell us what something looks like.

它們告訴我們某樣東西看起來怎麼樣。

They tell us what something feels like.

它們告訴我們某樣東西摸起來怎麼樣。

They tell us what something sounds like.

它們告訴我們某樣東西聽起來怎麼樣。

They tell us what something smells like.

它們告訴我們某樣東西聞起來怎麼樣。

They tell us what something tastes like.

它們告訴我們某樣東西吃起來怎麼樣。

Adjectives make writing more interesting.

形容詞會讓寫作更有趣、更生動。
